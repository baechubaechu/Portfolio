The Concept

Deary is an interactive AI diary created to resolve the friction of journaling.

Instead of 'writing' a diary, the user 'converses' with the AI, which weaves the responses into a sincere and cohesive diary entry.

Breaking away from rigid documentation, we redefined the journaling experience as a warm conversation to reflect on the day.

Core Experience

01.

Interactive Dialog

Fragmented thoughts are captured through natural conversation, removing the "blank page" pressure.

02.

AI Synthesis

Advanced LLM processing transforms chat logs into a cohesive, narrative diary entry.

03.

Bilingual Flow

Optimized for both Korean and English, ensuring linguistic nuances are preserved.
